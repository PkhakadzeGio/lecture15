What breakpoints should I use in 2022?

  • Mobile – 360 x 640; 375 x 667; 360 x 720.
  • Tablet – 768 x 1024.
  • iPhone – 375 x 812.
  • Laptop – 1366 x 768.
  • Desktop or high-resolution laptop – 1920 x 1080.
